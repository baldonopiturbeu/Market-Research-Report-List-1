<p><p>The truck drive axles market is segmented into single, tandem, and tridem types based on the number of axles providing drive power. Single axles support lighter loads and are often used in smaller trucks. Tandem axles consist of two axles working together, improving stability and load capacity, making them common in medium to heavy-duty trucks. Tridem axles incorporate three axles, further enhancing load distribution and traction, typically found in heavy transport and specialized applications for maximum payload efficiency.</p></p>
