<p><ul><li>Cellular Based Technology</li><li>DSRC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication market primarily includes two types: Cellular-Based Technology and Dedicated Short Range Communications (DSRC). Cellular-Based Technology utilizes existing cellular networks to facilitate real-time communication between vehicles, offering broader coverage and greater data capacity. In contrast, DSRC is a short-range, high-speed wireless communication protocol specifically designed for automotive applications, enabling low-latency interactions essential for safety-critical applications. Both technologies aim to enhance road safety, traffic management, and overall driving experience through improved connectivity among vehicles.</p></p>
