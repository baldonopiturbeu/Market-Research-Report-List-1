<p><p>The Reverse Shoulder System market includes various types of shoulder arthroplasty, notably Total Shoulder Arthroplasty and the Ream & Run procedure. Total Shoulder Arthroplasty involves replacing the glenohumeral joint with artificial components, enhancing mobility and reducing pain. In contrast, the Ream & Run technique focuses on resurfacing the joint, preserving bone and reducing the need for implants. Both methods aim to improve shoulder function and quality of life for patients with rotator cuff deficiencies or arthritis-related issues.</p></p>
