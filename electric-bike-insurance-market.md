<p><p>Electric bike insurance encompasses two primary market types: property insurance and accident insurance. Property insurance covers theft, damage, or loss of the electric bike itself, ensuring financial protection against unforeseen events. Conversely, accident insurance provides coverage for bodily injuries sustained by the rider in case of an accident, covering medical expenses and liability issues. Together, these insurance types help mitigate risks associated with riding electric bikes, promoting safer usage and financial security for owners.</p></p>
