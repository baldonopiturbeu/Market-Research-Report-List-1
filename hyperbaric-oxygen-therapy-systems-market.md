<p><p>The Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy Systems market comprises two main types: monoplace and multiplace chambers. Monoplace chambers are designed for single patients, providing a controlled environment for individual treatment, which enhances comfort and minimizes infection risk. In contrast, multiplace chambers accommodate multiple patients simultaneously, allowing healthcare professionals to administer treatment to several individuals at once, often beneficial in emergency situations. Both systems are essential in various clinical settings, catering to diverse patient needs and therapy requirements.</p></p>
