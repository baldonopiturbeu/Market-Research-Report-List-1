<p><p>The Steel Wire Tire Cord Market encompasses various tensile types, each defined by strength and applications. Normal Tensile (NT) cord is typically used for standard tires, offering adequate support. High Tensile (HT) cord provides enhanced strength, improving performance in heavier vehicles. Super Tensile (ST) cord is designed for high-performance tires, enabling better handling and durability. Ultra Tensile (UT) cord offers the highest strength and is used in specialized applications, such as racing or heavy-duty industrial tires, for exceptional resilience and safety.</p></p>
