<p><p>A Reciprocating Piston Compressor is a type of positive displacement compressor that uses a piston driven by a crankshaft to compress air or gas. It operates by drawing in gas through an intake valve during the downward stroke of the piston and then compressing it in an enclosed chamber on the upward stroke before discharging it through an outlet valve. This technology is widely used in various applications, including refrigeration, air conditioning, and industrial processes.</p><p>The Reciprocating Piston Compressor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period.
