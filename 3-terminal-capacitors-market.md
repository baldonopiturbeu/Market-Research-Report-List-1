<p><ul><li>Radial</li><li>SMD or SMT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3-terminal capacitor market comprises three primary types: radial, surface-mount device (SMD), and surface-mount technology (SMT) capacitors. Radial capacitors feature leads bent at the base for through-hole mounting, offering robust connections in bulkier applications. SMD capacitors are designed for automated assembly on printed circuit boards, providing a compact form factor for high-density applications. SMT capacitors are similar but typically refer to a broader category of components that adhere directly to the PCB surface, optimizing space and enhancing performance.</p></p>
