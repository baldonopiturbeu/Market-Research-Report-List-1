<p><p>The osteochondral implants market comprises several types, primarily focusing on allografts and screw and plate systems. Allografts involve transplanting donor tissue to repair damaged cartilage and underlying bone, promoting natural healing. Screw and plate systems, on the other hand, provide mechanical support and stabilization for osteochondral lesions, facilitating recovery and integration of grafts. These methods are crucial in orthopedic treatments, addressing conditions like cartilage defects and enhancing joint function through advanced surgical techniques and materials.</p></p>
