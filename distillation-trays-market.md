<p><p>The distillation trays market includes several types, each designed for specific separation processes. Bubble cap trays feature caps that enhance vapor contact, maximizing efficiency. Sieve trays have perforated plates, allowing vapors to pass through liquid, providing a straightforward design for effective mass transfer. Valve trays incorporate movable valves that adjust to varying flow rates, offering flexibility and improved performance under changing conditions. Collectively, these trays optimize distillation efficiency in chemical processing by enhancing vapor-liquid interactions.</p></p>
