<p><ul><li>Mirrorless System</li><li>Fixed Mirror System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro-single camera market comprises two main types: mirrorless systems and fixed mirror systems. Mirrorless cameras eliminate the mirror mechanism found in traditional DSLRs, allowing for lighter, compact designs while maintaining high image quality. They offer interchangeable lenses and advanced features for enthusiasts and professionals. Fixed mirror systems, on the other hand, integrate a fixed mirror in a compact design, providing a balance between user-friendliness and performance, often aimed at casual photographers seeking simplicity without sacrificing too much capability.</p></p>
