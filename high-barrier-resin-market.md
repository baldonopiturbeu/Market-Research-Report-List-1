<p><p>The high barrier resin market comprises various types, including Polyethylene Naphthalate (PEN), Polyvinylidene Chloride (PVDC), Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH), and others. PEN is known for its excellent moisture and gas barrier properties, enhancing packaging durability. PVDC provides robust oxygen and aroma barriers, making it popular in food packaging. Other resins may include combinations or new polymers designed for specific applications, expanding their utility across industries.</p></p>
